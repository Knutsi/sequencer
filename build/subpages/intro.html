Introduction

Welcome to this experiment, and thank you for participating. Completion will take around 20 minutes. You will be presented with a short introduction video and three medical cases with tools to arrive at one or more diagnosis for each case. After each case, you will fill out a form to evaluate the medical side of the case given and provide some details about your background.

Note: The information collected here is processed anonymously, and does not contain your name, email or other information that can be used to trace it back to you. Please do not provide personal information in any free text field, as your responses may be made available to the public. Invitations to this experiment will be numerous, and age/profession will not identify you.
